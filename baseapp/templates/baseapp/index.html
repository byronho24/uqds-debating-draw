Welcome to the UQDS Internals Tab!

Lead Development by Byron Ho, Project Design by Liam Bond.

For the first time ever, we’ve developed a custom solution for the UQDS Internals Competition. This web-page is your go-to site for every week of Internals 2019.


How To Use the App

The app is separated into sections:

  1. Team Registration

    Before Internals, speakers must register to debate together. Here, you can choose a team name and choose your team members. Importantly,

    • Each person must be a 2019 UQDS memeber to be eligible to be in a team
    • Each person can only be in one team
    • Each team only needs to be registered once
    • Each team can have up to 5 members.

  2. Attendance form

    Before each week of the competition, you must register your attendance via the attendance form. Select your team, then select who’s here this week. If you’d prefer to judge, simply check the checkbox. You can change and resubmit your attendance form as many times as you like, as only the most recent submission will count.

  3. Draw

    This is where the VP (Internals) will publish the draw, once it has been finalised. You can click on a debate to reveal speakers and additional information.


    4. This app is currently in testing phase.
